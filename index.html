This is how to tie your shoes:)

Being able to tie your shoes is very important to your sucess as a working individual and can say alot about the kind of person you are. Thankfully it is also a very easy skill to learn.

To start, take the two differant strings in each hand and cross them to pull them tight. Next make a loop with one of the strings and wrap the other string around that loop. Now you take the string that is wrapped around the loop and stick it inti the whole youve now created in the middle. Pull the strings tight to make it complete. Congrats you did it, now go out there and make a differance!!!
